What's new in this release

(As a last gift?!? It's up to you!) I have made some minor code improvements and I have added the two most requested features:

  1. You will never get annoyed again by the “Video paused. Continue watching?” prompt that appears after approximately 35-40 minutes of inactivity when a long video or a playlist is playing in a background tab.
  2. The extension will prevent the page from jumping to the top when you will click timestamp links in comments if the player is pinned.

What has been fixed

Note: I cannot fix odd issues that have been reported without clear instructions to reproduce them.

  • Mac and Linux: The player's menu appears when the right mouse button is pressed to control the volume level.
  • Multi-touch trackpad: The pinch in (zoom out) and pinch out (zoom in) gestures over the video modify the playback speed instead of zooming in/out.
  • Video aspect ratios different than 16:9 (i.e. 4:3, 18:9, 21:9, and others) not supported by the large player mode.
  • Player, progress bar, and other elements not correctly resized when the window is minimised/maximised while the large player mode is active.
  • Tollbar not visible when it should.
  • Toolbar visible when YouTube's mini player is enabled.
  • Cinema mode still active and preventing page scrolling when YouTube's mini player is enabled.
  • Playback speed buttons “-” and “+” reversed when the “Reverse the mouse wheel direction...” feature is enabled.
  • Dark themes issues.
  • Dark theme still partially visible and causing issues when disabled while no YouTube tab was open.
  • Old YouTube layout: The player's menu appears when the right mouse button is pressed while the cursor is over the video's black borders (not the black borders visible when YouTube's Theater mode is active).
